CORONA VIRUS FACTS

How does COVID-19 Spread?

The virus that causes COVID-19 is mainly transmitted through droplets when an infected coughs,sneezes, or exhales. These droplets are too heavy to hang in the air. It can be infected by breathing in the virus or by touching a contaminated surface and then your eyes, nose or mouth.

What are the Symptoms of COVID-19?

The most common symptoms of COVID-19 are fever, dry cough and may affect some patients include aches and pains, headache, conjunctivitis, sore throat, diarrhea, loss of taste or smell or discoloration of fingers or toes. Some people become infected but only have very mild symptoms.
